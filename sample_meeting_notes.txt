=== Key Decisions ===

Decision: Adopt OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for Authentication
- Approved by: Sarah Chen
- Date: August 15, 2024
- Rationale: After evaluating OAuth 2.0, SAML, and custom JWT, we chose OAuth 2.0 with PKCE. 
  Key factors: better mobile SDK support, lower implementation complexity, strong security 
  guarantees, and aligns with Auth0 capabilities. SAML was rejected because we don't need 
  enterprise-only features yet. Custom JWT would require too much security engineering effort.
- Related Project: Auth System Rebuild

Decision: Pause Analytics Migration Until Q4
- Approved by: Sarah Chen  
- Date: September 10, 2024
- Rationale: Engineering capacity is constrained by SOC 2 remediation and auth system work.
  Since Mixpanel contract doesn't expire until Q1 2025, there's no cost pressure. We'll 
  re-evaluate scope and timeline in Q4 when we have more capacity.
- Related Project: Analytics Migration

Decision: Select Drata as SOC 2 Compliance Vendor
- Approved by: Elena Rodriguez
- Date: July 5, 2024
- Rationale: Evaluated Drata vs Vanta. Drata won on integration breadth (covers our AWS, 
  Okta, and GitHub stack natively) and stronger continuous monitoring. Vanta was 18% cheaper 
  but required more manual evidence collection. Total cost of ownership favored Drata.
- Related Project: Compliance Audit
