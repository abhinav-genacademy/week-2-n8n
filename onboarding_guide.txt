Pre-boarding Checklist
Within 48 hours of receiving your offer acceptance, you will receive emails from:
1. IT (it-setup@acmecorp.com) — to choose your laptop model and operating system preference (Mac or PC)
2. HR (hr@acmecorp.com) — to complete I-9, W-4, direct deposit, and benefits enrollment
3. Your manager — a welcome note and first-week schedule

Action Items Before Day 1:
- Complete the I-9 and W-4 forms in Workday (deadline: Day 1 of employment — federal requirement)
- Select your benefits during the 30-day new hire enrollment window (health, dental, vision, 401k)
- Review your offer letter and flag any discrepancies to hr@acmecorp.com
- Set up your ACME email (instructions in the IT setup email) and complete MFA enrollment
- Download Slack, Zoom, and the ACME VPN client (GlobalProtect) on your personal device before Day 1

Equipment: Your laptop and peripherals will be shipped to your home address (or available for pickup at your office location). Expect delivery 2-3 business days before your start date. If it does not arrive, contact IT immediately.

PART 2: YOUR FIRST DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 What to Expect
First-day orientation runs 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM EST (virtual for remote employees, in-person for office-based). The session covers: company history and culture, org chart overview, IT security essentials, and a tour of key tools.

2.2 Tools You Will Use From Day 1
- Workday: Your HR system of record. Manage time-off requests, performance reviews, pay stubs, and personal info.
- Slack: Primary internal communication. Join #announcements, #general, and your team channel on Day 1. Avoid over-relying on email for internal conversations.
- Zoom: Video meetings. All All-Hands and 1:1s default to Zoom.
- Jira: Project and task tracking (Engineering, Product, Design, Data teams). Other teams may use Asana or Monday.
- Confluence: Internal wiki and knowledge base. Search here before asking a colleague.
- Google Workspace: Docs, Sheets, Slides, Drive for document collaboration.
- Expensify: Submit expense reports within 30 days of incurring the expense.
- Okta: Single Sign-On for all ACME applications. If a tool asks for a password, try "Sign in with Okta" first.

2.3 Setting Up Your Workspace
IT will pre-configure your laptop with standard software. First-time login requires your ACME email and Okta password. After logging in:
1. Connect to the ACME VPN (GlobalProtect) — required before accessing internal systems
2. Complete endpoint security enrollment (CrowdStrike) — IT will prompt automatically
3. Verify your Slack, Zoom, and Google Workspace access
4. Complete the mandatory Day 1 security training module in the LMS (30 minutes)

PART 3: FIRST 30 DAYS — LEARN AND CONNECT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Mandatory Training (Complete by Day 30)
All new employees must complete the following training modules in the Learning Management System (LMS at learn.acmecorp.com):
- Information Security Awareness (1 hour) — Day 1
- Code of Conduct and Ethics (45 minutes) — Week 1
- Anti-Harassment and Workplace Respect (1 hour) — Week 1
- Data Privacy Fundamentals (45 minutes) — Week 2
- Anti-Money Laundering Basics (30 minutes, if applicable to role) — Week 2
- Insider Trading Prevention (30 minutes, Finance/Legal/Product roles) — Week 2
Incomplete training by Day 30 will be escalated to your manager.

3.2 Meet Your Team
Your manager will schedule a series of intro meetings:
- 1:1 with your manager (Day 1): Discuss role expectations, immediate priorities, and communication preferences
- Team introductions (Week 1): Meet your immediate team in a group setting
- Cross-functional intros (Weeks 2-3): At least 5 meetings with stakeholders in adjacent teams
- "Coffee Chat" program: HR will match you with 3 peer mentors across the company during your first month

3.3 Understanding the Org
The ACME organizational structure has five divisions:
- Product & Technology: Product Management, Engineering, Data Science, Design
- Go-To-Market: Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Business Development
- Finance & Operations: Finance, Accounting, Legal, Compliance, IT, HR
- Strategy: Corporate Strategy, M&A, Investor Relations
- Delivery: Professional Services, Implementation, Support

Each division is led by a C-suite executive who reports to the CEO. The Executive Leadership Team (ELT) holds a weekly Monday meeting. Key decisions and strategy updates are communicated via the monthly All-Hands (last Thursday of each month, 3 PM EST).

3.4 Setting Goals (OKRs)
Within your first 30 days, work with your manager to set your first-quarter OKRs (Objectives and Key Results). ACME runs OKRs on a quarterly cycle:
- Q1: January-March | Planning in December
- Q2: April-June | Planning in March
- Q3: July-September | Planning in June
- Q4: October-December | Planning in September

OKRs are set at company, team, and individual levels. Individual OKRs should connect to team OKRs. Aim for 3-5 objectives with 2-4 measurable key results each. OKRs are visible to your manager and their management chain.

PART 4: DAYS 31-60 — CONTRIBUTE AND BUILD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 Taking Ownership
By the end of Month 2, you should be: leading at least one project or workstream independently, making decisions within your role's scope without requiring manager approval for each, and attending and contributing to relevant meetings rather than observing.

4.2 Expense Reimbursements
Business expenses (travel, software, supplies) are reimbursed through Expensify:
- Submit within 30 days of the expense date; late submissions may be denied
- Attach receipts for all items above $25
- For travel expenses, reference the Travel Policy (intranet) for per diem rates and booking rules
- Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll cycle after approval

Software purchases above $500 require IT pre-approval via the software request form on the IT portal.

4.3 Learning and Development
Your $1,500 annual L&D stipend is available from Day 1 (prorated for the calendar year). Use it for:
- External courses and certifications (Coursera, Udemy, AWS, GMAT prep — any professional topic)
- Industry conferences (travel costs covered separately via the Travel Policy)
- Books and professional subscriptions

Submit L&D requests in Workday under "Learning Requests." Manager approval is required for items above $500 or conferences requiring travel. ACME also has enterprise licenses for: LinkedIn Learning, O'Reilly, and Pluralsight — available at no extra cost.

PART 5: DAYS 61-90 — ACCELERATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1 90-Day Check-In
Your manager will conduct a formal 90-day check-in. This is not a performance review — it is a two-way conversation covering: what is going well, what needs adjustment, whether your role matches expectations, and what support you need in the next quarter. Prepare a written self-assessment in advance (template in Workday).

5.2 After Day 90
- You are now eligible to apply for remote work arrangements (subject to HR policy — see HR Manual Section 3.1)
- Your 401(k) contributions begin accruing company match
- You become eligible to participate in the Employee Referral Program ($3,000 bonus for successful referrals; paid 90 days after the referred employee starts)
